DNS & Domain
Health Checker

Your domain's DNS settings quietly control whether your website loads, whether your email gets delivered, and how easy you are to impersonate. Enter a domain to get a plain-language health check — resolution, nameservers, email security, DNSSEC and more.

🔒 No server of ours is involved. Your browser asks public DNS resolvers (Cloudflare, then Google) directly — exactly what any computer does to look up a website. We never see your query, and nothing is stored. You can watch the lookups in your browser's developer tools.
